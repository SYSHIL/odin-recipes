Masala dosa


Masala dosa is a variation of the popular South Indian dosa, which has its origins in Udupi cuisine of Karnataka.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Urad dal
  • Chana dal
  • Rice flour

Instructions:

  • Blend the lentil and rice batter
  • Ferment the batter
  • Prepare the savory potato masala filling
  • Assemble the complete masala dosa
  • Fold the dosa and serve
